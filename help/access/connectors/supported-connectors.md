# Supported connectors

A connector can work with a database, an Excel file, a CSV file, a web service, or basically anything that is capable of providing the data. Datagrok currently supports over 30 different connectors, and the list is quickly growing. Most of Datagrokâ€™s data connectors are open-sourced and could be found on [GitHub](https://github.com/datagrok-ai/public/tree/master/connectors) (MIT license).
